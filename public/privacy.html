2. Device Permissions

To provide our core carpooling service, the App requests specific device permissions. You can revoke these at any time via your device settings, though this will restrict App functionality.

  • Precise Location (Foreground only):
    • Why we need it: We collect your GPS location while the App is open and in active use — to match you with nearby rides, show pick-up/drop-off points, and calculate trip distances.
    • Background Location: The App does not currently access your location in the background. Location tracking stops as soon as you close or minimise the App. If background location is introduced in a future release, we will request additional explicit consent and update this policy before enabling it.
  • Camera & Media Library: Requested exclusively so you can upload a profile picture or photos of your vehicle. Photos remain on your device until you explicitly choose to upload them.
  • Microphone (Voice Notes): Requested exclusively to allow you to record and send in-app audio voice messages to your matched driver/rider. We do not listen to or record background audio at any time.
  • Push Notifications: To alert you about ride requests, approvals, arrivals, chat messages, and event updates.

3. User-Generated Content (UGC) and Safety Guidelines

SportConnect maintains a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content or abusive users. By using the app, you agree not to submit content that is illegal, defamatory, abusive, or discriminatory.

  • Content Moderation: We reserve the right to review, monitor, and delete any UGC that violates our Terms of Service.
  • Blocking and Reporting: Users can flag inappropriate messages or block abusive users directly within the app. Upon receiving a report, our moderation team will review the flagged account within 24 hours and take appropriate action.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We only share your data under the following conditions:

  • With Other Users: When you join a carpool, your profile picture, first name, and live location (during the active ride only) are visible to the driver and other matched riders for safety and logistics.
  • Service Providers (Processors): We share technical data with trusted third-party providers bound by strict data processing agreements:
    • Stripe: For secure payment processing and driver identity verification (Stripe Connect). Stripe acts as a data processor for card payments and as a data controller for Connect KYC data.
    • Google / Firebase: For secure database hosting, user authentication, push notifications, crash reporting, and analytics.
    • Apple App Store / Google Play: For in-app purchase validation and subscription management.
    • OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine — router.project-osrm.org): For route calculation and turn-by-turn navigation. Route queries (start/end coordinates) are sent to the OSRM public API. No personal identifiers are attached to these requests.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of a user, we will share data with law enforcement.

5. International Data Transfers

SportConnect is primarily directed at European users. However, we use cloud infrastructure provided by Google (Firebase), Stripe, and Apple, some of whose servers may be located in the United States. In accordance with CNIL guidelines, all transfers of personal data outside the EU are safeguarded by the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (DPF) and European Commission-approved Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

6. Data Retention Policy

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined:

  • Account Data: Retained for the lifetime of your account.
  • Ride History & Location Data: Precise trip logs are anonymised or deleted after 12 months.
  • Financial Records: Invoices, payment history, and tax-related transaction logs are retained for up to 10 years as required by French/EU accounting laws.
  • In-App Purchase Records: Retained for the duration required by Apple/Google platform policies and applicable tax law.

7. Your GDPR Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access & Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update inaccurate profile information within the app.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can delete your account and associated data.
  • Right to Object & Restrict: You can object to data processing based on legitimate interests.

8. Account and Data Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account at any time. When you request deletion, your profile, active rides, chats, and images will be permanently erased within 30 days (excluding financial records retained by legal obligation).

  • In-App: Navigate to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  • On the Web: Visit our Account Deletion Form to process erasure without reinstalling the app.

9. Children's Privacy

SportConnect is limited to individuals aged 18 and older. The App enforces this at registration by requiring users to confirm they meet the age requirement; accounts identified as belonging to minors are deleted immediately. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Premium Features and In-App Purchases

SportConnect offers optional Premium subscriptions and one-time purchases processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. When you subscribe or make a purchase:

  • Payment is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not receive or store your card number, billing address, or banking details.
  • We receive a receipt/entitlement token that confirms your subscription status.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store or Play Store account settings.
